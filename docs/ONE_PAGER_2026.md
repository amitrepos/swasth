**1. Photo** / **फोटो**
- Patient takes a photo of their BP or glucose machine display
- Mareez BP ya sugar machine ki photo leta hai

**2. WhatsApp** / **व्हाट्सएप**
- Send the photo to Swasth's WhatsApp number (+91-XXX-XXX-XXXX)
- Swasth ke WhatsApp number pe bhej do

**3. Auto-log** / **अपने आप दर्ज**
- AI reads the reading in 10 seconds, logs it, confirms in Hindi
- AI 10 second mein reading pehchan ke log karta hai, Hindi mein confirm

**4. Family report** / **परिवार को रिपोर्ट**
- Weekly WhatsApp to NRI child — "Mom's BP stable this week" / "Mom ka BP is week stable"
- Doctor gets monthly summary on request

## Design notes (for whoever lays this out)

- **Font:** Sans-serif, 11-12pt body, 14pt headings. Hindi: use Noto Sans Devanagari or Mangal.
- **Colors:** 2-color maximum. Swasth brand teal (#006D77) + warm grey (#595959). No gradients.
- **White space:** ≥ 15mm margins. Don't cram — doctors hate dense flyers.
- **Icons:** Use simple line icons for the 4-step flow (camera, message bubble, checkmark, family).
- **Images:** One real photo of a Bihar aunty using WhatsApp (stock or actual, with consent). No stock-looking "elderly couple with tablet" images.
- **CTA:** QR code at minimum 25mm × 25mm so it's scannable from arm's length.
- **Back footer:** "© 2026 Neo Financial Technologies Pvt Ltd. Swasth is a product of Neo Financial Technologies."

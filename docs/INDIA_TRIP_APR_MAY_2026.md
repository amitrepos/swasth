## 3. The "hospital doesn't allow" reframe (use this every time)

**Their objection:**
> "Main recommend nahi kar sakta — hospital policy hai."

**Your reframe (memorize word-for-word):**
> "Sir, bilkul samjha. Hospital ke patients ke liye nahi keh raha. Aap 3 aise patients ko jaante hain — personal practice mein, ya jaan-pehchaan ke — jinko Type 2 diabetes ya BP hai? Bas unke number de dijiye, main direct baat kar lunga. Aapka naam use nahi karunga agar aap nahi chahte. 2 hafte baad aapko weekly report bhejunga ki unka BP/sugar kaisa hai. Bas itna."

**Why this works:**
- Removes hospital as the blocker.
- Small ask (3 patients, not 50).
- You do the work, not them.
- They get data back — value to them, not just extraction.
- Anonymity option removes risk.

If they still decline after this → disinterest, not policy. Move on politely.

## 6. The 8-question structured interview protocol

Same 8 questions, same order, score each 1-5 on a tracking sheet. Consistency makes data comparable.

1. **Patient volume:** Kitne diabetic / BP patients aapko daily / weekly aate hain?
2. **Follow-up reality:** Inme se kitne 3 mahine ke baad wapas aate hain? Kya aap track karte hain?
3. **Current pain:** Follow-up mein sabse badi challenge kya hai?
4. **Data flow:** Kya aap patient ke BP/sugar ki history dekhna chahenge ghar ki readings ke sath?
5. **Open to trial:** Agar 3 patients ko main setup kar doon, aap 2 hafte tak unki reports dekhenge?
6. **Family involvement:** Aapke patients ke bacche videsh mein rehte hain? Kitne percent?
7. **Payment willingness:** Aapke patients ki family Rs 500/month dene ko tayyar hogi is service ke liye, agar doctor recommend kare?
8. **What would make you say yes:** "Sir, aap kya chahenge ki iska use ho? Kya feature hona chahiye?"

**Score interpretation:**
- Q5 "yes" + Q7 "maybe/yes" = GREEN lead. Follow up within 48h.
- Q5 "no" but Q3-4 engaged = YELLOW. Try one-more-meeting.
- Q1-4 disengaged = RED. Thank, close, don't chase.

## 7. Scripts and dialogues

> **Principle:** Open in Hindi. Switch to English only if they do. Small ask. Time-boxed. No VC/funding talk. No "AI-powered" language.

### 7.1 WhatsApp to relative-who-is-a-doctor (Hindi)

> Namaste [Bhai / Didi / Mamaji],
>

>

>

### 7.6 Chamber meeting — first 90 seconds (Hindi)

**Arrival:** Arrive 15 min early. Namaste compounder: "Dr. [intro giver] ne bheja hai, main [your name]." Sit. Observe. When called, enter, stand behind chair, Namaste.

> "Namaste sir. Aapka time lene ke liye shukriya. [Intro giver name] ne bahut tareef ki thi aapki — unhone bataya aap bahut acche doctor hain diabetic patients ke liye."

**Wait for "baithiye." Then:**

> "Sir, main sirf 15 minute lunga. Agar patient aa jaye beech mein, aap handle kar lijiye, main wait kar lunga.
>
> Pehle aapki practice ke baare mein thoda samjhna chahta hoon — aapke paas kitne diabetic patients aate hain roz?"

**3 minutes of Q1-Q4 from §6. Listen. Don't pitch yet.**

**After Q4 — 90-second pitch:**

> "Sir, jo main bana raha hoon wo bilkul simple hai. [PHONE IN HAND] — patient apna BP ya sugar ghar se log karta hai, har din. Unki family — aksar videsh mein hoti hai — unko weekly report automatically jaati hai. Aap bhi chahen toh aapko summary milegi.
>
> [Swipe through demo — 60 seconds max.]
>
> Hospital ka app nahi hai — patient ka apna app hai, ghar mein. Aap recommend nahi kar rahe. Bas 3 patients ko main 2 hafte ke liye setup kar doon. Reading dekhiye. Agar useful lage, hum baat karenge agey. Nahi lage, band."

**Then Q5-Q8. Then close.**

### 7.7 Closing small ask (after chamber pitch)

> "Sir, ek specific request — aap 3 aise patients ko jaante hain jo Type 2 diabetic ya BP ke hain, beech-umar ya buzurg, jinke bache shahar ke bahar ya videsh mein rehte hain? Bas unke number de dijiye, main direct unse baat karunga. 2 hafte main aapko report bhejunga ki kya hua. Aapko kuch bhi nahi karna.
>
> Aur sir — agar aapka time allow kare — hum ek chhota honorarium dete hain advisor doctors ko, Rs 5,000 per meeting ya per patient, aapki marzi kaise structure kare."

**Wait. Don't fill silence.**

If yes: "Shukriya sir. Main aaj raat ek WhatsApp message bhejunga — aap 3 patient numbers share kar dein jab time ho. Koi rush nahi."

If no/vague: "Bilkul theek hai sir. Ek week baad phir message karunga. Shukriya." Leave one-pager on desk. Leave.

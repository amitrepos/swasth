Delete Your Swasth Account

App: Swasth — Health Tracker (health.swasth.app)
Developer: Swasth
Effective date: 18 April 2026
Last updated: 18 April 2026

This page explains how to permanently delete your Swasth account and all the personal and health data associated with it. You can initiate deletion from inside the app or by contacting us via email — both paths result in the same permanent removal of your data.

This right is guaranteed under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (Section 12 — Right to Erasure) and the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011.

1. Delete from inside the Swasth app (recommended)

If you have the Swasth app installed and can log in, this is the fastest way:

  1. Open the Swasth app on your Android device.
  2. Log in with your registered email and password.
  3. Tap the Profile icon in the bottom navigation.
  4. Scroll to the bottom of the Profile screen.
  5. Tap Delete Account (shown in red).
  6. Read the confirmation dialog carefully, then tap Confirm Delete.

Once confirmed, your account is deactivated immediately, you are signed out, and a server-side deletion process removes all your data permanently within 7 days (see Section 4 for the retention schedule).

2. Delete by email (if you cannot use the app)

If you have uninstalled the app, lost access to your device, forgotten your password, or simply prefer email, send a deletion request to:

Email: swasth.admin@gmail.com
Subject: Account Deletion Request
In the email body, please include:
  • The email address you used to register with Swasth
  • Your full name as entered in your Swasth profile (for identity verification)
  • A sentence confirming you want your account and data permanently deleted

We will respond within 3 working days to confirm your identity, and complete the deletion within 7 working days of identity confirmation. You will receive a final confirmation email when deletion is complete.

3. What gets deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (name, age, gender, phone number, medical conditions, current medications)
  • All health readings you logged (blood pressure, blood glucose, pulse, SpO₂, steps)
  • All meal logs and associated photos
  • All AI chat history and generated health insights
  • Profile access entries linking family members or caregivers to your account
  • Doctor-patient link records tied to your account
  • Email verification records, OTP logs, and authentication tokens
  • Any device IDs, session identifiers, and in-app preferences stored against your account

4. What is kept, and for how long

A small amount of data may be retained for a limited period, strictly for the legitimate purposes listed below. None of this retained data is used for any other purpose, and it is permanently destroyed at the end of each retention window.

Data Retention after account deletion Why it is kept
Encrypted database backups containing your data Up to 30 days Disaster-recovery rolling backups. Backups older than 30 days are automatically overwritten.
Security and access audit logs (timestamp, anonymised user ID, action type) Up to 180 days Required under Indian cyber-incident reporting guidelines. Does not contain health readings or personal details.
Transactional records (account creation and deletion timestamps) Up to 3 years Required for legal/regulatory compliance and tax-law recordkeeping. Does not contain health data.

5. Partial deletion (delete some data, keep your account)

If you want to delete specific records without deleting your entire account, the Swasth app supports this:

  • Delete a specific health reading: open History → long-press or swipe on the reading → Delete.
  • Delete a specific meal log: open History → Meals Only filter → swipe on the meal → Delete.
  • Remove a family member from your profile: open Profile → Care Circle → tap the member → Remove Access.
  • Unlink your doctor: open Profile → Linked Doctors → tap the doctor → Revoke Link.
  • Clear AI chat history: open AI Doctor → menu → Clear Conversation.

6. After deletion — what to expect

  • You will not be able to log in with the deleted email.
  • You can re-register with the same email at any time in the future — the new account will have no historical data from the deleted one.
  • Family members who had access to your profile will stop seeing it in their Care Circle.
  • Your linked doctor (if any) will see your link as "revoked" in their dashboard.

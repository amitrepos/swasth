Common questions

What people ask us first.

What if Mom doesn't have a smartphone, or doesn't use one?+
The Swasth coach logs her readings during the daily call. She doesn't have to touch a screen. As we roll out voice AI in late 2026, she'll just speak — no app, no typing.
Will a real doctor actually sign the weekly summary?+
Yes. Every week, an HPR-verified physician reviews her trends and signs the one-page summary. You receive it on WhatsApp. It's a real medical record you can share with any specialist, anywhere.
When does Swasth actually launch?+
We're in pre-launch right now. Join the waitlist and we'll email you the moment Swasth opens for early access (estimated end of May 2026). Waitlist members get the first month free. After that, ₹999/month per parent, cancel anytime.
Is my parent's data safe?+
Yes. Data stored in India (AWS Mumbai). ABDM-compliant. Aadhaar encrypted, never plaintext. DPDPA 2023 compliant. Only Mom and the family she invites can see her health data.
I'm in India, not abroad — is Swasth for me?+
Yes. Anyone whose parent lives alone — whether you're in Berlin, Boston, Bangalore, or Bhubaneswar — gets the same daily peace of mind.
